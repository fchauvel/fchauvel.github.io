Biography

2016-08-17 · 224 words · 2 minute read
about-me

Hi, I’m Franck! Enthusiasm and curiosity have turned me into a “Swiss knife”. Not only do I design, manage and sell research & innovation projects in Software Engineering, but I still write code!

What am I looking for?

I am a programmer researching for better ways to design and implement self-adaptive systems, that is systems that automatically adjust to their environment. I am looking for opportunities to evaluate research ideas in industrial settings, especially around Cloud technologies.

My current objective is to detect, at design-time, performances issues due to interactions between feedback loops, using simulation of architectural models.

What am I doing?

I am currently ‘research scientist’ in SINTEF ICT where I joined the SSD group. Our activities focus on model-driven engineering applied to various areas such as embedded systems, cloud computing or scientific computing. I contributed to several international research projects, such as the FP7 projects MODAClouds, DIVERSIFY or DREAMS as well as Norwegian national projects such as CIRRUS.

What I used to do?

Formerly, I spent almost 2 years, in Beijing (China), where I joined the SAM group (Software Architecture and Middleware Group) of SEI in the Peking University. I hold a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of South Brittany (France) since 2008, and a Master degree in Computer Science from the University of Rennes 1 (France) since 2003.
